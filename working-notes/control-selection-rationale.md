# Control Statement Exercise

## AC-3 - Access Enforcement
The system checks who you are and only lets you do what your job requires.

## SC-7 - Boundary Protection
a. Monitor/control all inbound/outbound traffic across the external layers of the network and within sensitive systems.
b. Segment the network for public and private use.
c. Follow existing security and privacy architecture to ensure outbound traffic is only exiting through approved endpoints.

## AU-2 - Event Logging
a. Identify what can be logged.
b. Find out from other people in the org which logs should be collected.
c. Specify what events will trigger the logs.
d. Explain how those logs can be used when an incident occurs to explain what's happened to the system.
e. Periodically review and update the logging system as needed.

## SI-4 - System Monitoring
a. Monitor for IoCs and attacks using org-defined objectives and any unauthorized connections.
b. Use org-defined techniques and objectives to identify unauthorized use.
c. Use internal or external monitoring systems for DLP and monitor chosen points of communications.
d. Analyze the events.
e. Adjust the level of activity based on perceived/realized threats.
f. Get legal support.
g. Provide logs to the personnel who need to review them. 

## IA-2 - Identification and Authentication (Organizational Users)
Employees, contractors and researchers must be uniquely identified, authenticated, and authorized to operate within the system, and to have services operate on their behalf. **WRONG** No need for authorization. That's a different control.

Corrected: Employees, contractors, and researchers must identify themselves and be authenticated to operate or use services within the system.
