# Executive Summary

**System:** Logistics Tracking System (LTS)
**Date:** March 2026

## System Purpose

LTS provides real-time tracking and documentation of high-value assets in transit using AWS GovCloud.

## Data and Impact Level

The system handles CUI and PII at a MEDIUM impact level.

## Controls Selected

Five NIST 800-53 controls were selected: access enforcement (AC-3), boundary protection (SC-7), audit logging (AU-2), system monitoring (SI-4), and identification (IA-2). These represent the controls most directly implemented by the LTS team.

## POA&M Status

Five findings were identified: two High, two Medium, and one Low. Remediation plans are in place with target completion dates between May and June 2026.

## Recommendation

All other controls are fully implemented. LTS is recommended for authorization with remediation in progress.
